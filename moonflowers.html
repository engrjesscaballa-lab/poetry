Moonflowers

Moonflowers, white as snow,
You've seen him passing here I know.
Does the night feel warmer, too,
When he smiles the way he do?

Moonflowers, tell me true,
Do you bloom brighter when he's in view?
Do you hear my heart's small beat
When our shadows almost meet?

Moonflowers, keep my secret near,
Hold it close so no one hears,
But if he should come this way,
Shine for him, and help him stay.

Moonflowers, soft and kind,
You've watched the truth I've tried to hide.
If the wind should carry them far,
Send them to where he is, star by star!

Moonflowers, don't let go,
Guard my hope in your silver glow,
'Til the night turns into day
And he's no longer far away.
Julian Valdez
