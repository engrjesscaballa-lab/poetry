Why Do I Keep Walking at Night?

Why do I keep walking at night
And shut myself come morning light?
Is it the stillness of the air
Something the day could not compare?
Or can it be the vast darkness
Hugging all things in its caress?
All things that come the light of morn,
Can either bring bliss or doom.
Silence, darkness covers them all,
That makes the night more bearable.
So, dear friends, tell me honestly,
From what you've gathered out of me,
Am I a coward or a knight
To prefer walking in the night?
Julian Valdez
08.27.2018
