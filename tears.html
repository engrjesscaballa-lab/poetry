Midnight Tears

The midnight seeps through curtains thin,
A quiet room, yet loud within.
Her dreams had called her mother's name,
A fleeting glimpse, a tender frame.

But dreams dissolve as dawn delays,
Leaving echoes of better days.
Her chest feels heavy, her tears take flight,
Waking her softly in the silent night.

She clutches the air where love once stayed,
A bond too strong to ever fade.
Through midnight tears, she whispers low,
"I miss you more than you'll ever know."
Julian Valdez
