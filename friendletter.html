To A Friend

If today I die, do not mourn for me.
Wallow not in despair, for I am free.
Life has been a thrilling journey, don't grieve
Know while I'm gone, I didn't really leave.

I am the gentle whisper in the breeze,
I am the wild flowers swinging with ease,
I am with the sweet songs of robins,
I am with the gentle-flowing river.

So if you are overwhelmed by silence,
And dearly wishes your old friend's presence,
Travel to the hills where we used to be
And observe nature, you will be with me.
Julian Valdez
10.21.2019
