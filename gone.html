Gone

Where did they go, the dragonflies
We used to chase back then?
Gone, gone with our laughter
Swept by the distant wind.

Where did they go, the fireflies
Who adorned many a dark night?
Gone, gone with our dreams
Which flickered and died.

Where did they go, the crickets
Whose chorus filled night airs past?
Gone, gone with our love
Never to come back.
Julian Valdez
07.06.2018
