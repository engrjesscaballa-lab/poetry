If Only

If only there was a bridge
From Earth to Heaven
And if only there were cars
Which could take me there,
I'd follow you, grandfather,
And there hug you tighter
Than I've ever done before.
I'd play hide-and-seek with you
Like we used to do.
Or I'd sit on your lap
While you sing the old tune
You used to sing me years ago.
Or should you like me to,
I'd dance in front of you.
Yes, for you I'd try to
'Cause I know you'd always appreciate it so.
I'd fetch you from heaven on my graduation
So that it's you who'd pin me my ribbon,
Take you home on my debut
That my last dance shall be you,
And bring you on my wedding day
And see the beautiful woman I'd come to be.
So many things I long to do
And so many stories I long to tell
That if only there was a bridge
From Earth to Heaven
And if only there were cars
Which could take me there.
Julian Valdez
10.16.2012
To my dear Itay, Manuel Caballa y Banario, taken from us too soon and missed more than words can say. Your gentle spirit was truly one of a kind and we love you.
