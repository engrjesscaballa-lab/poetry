The Bubble Popped

The bubble popped.
Found my eyes blank. Captivated.
How long had it been this time?
A minute or two? No clue.
Fudge! He noticed!
For when he raised those black orbs,
They found mine.
That was why the bubble popped.
With a jolt, I looked away.
"Try to look innocent," I say,
Though this thing in my chest,
Goes 'thug-thug-thugging' so loudly.
This was supposed to be a secret,
This feeling...
Julian Valdez
10.11.2016
To that one unforgettable college professor — CG.
