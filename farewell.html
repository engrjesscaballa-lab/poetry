Farewell

Sunset has come silently
For you that's loved so dearly,
And though I wish that you'd stay,
I know our God's will I could not sway.

I left home with your smiling face
And a hug so tight I couldn't breathe.
Had I known that would be the last,
I wouldn't have moved free too fast.

There are so many things I would like to say
But alas, words are failing me today.
I just hope wherever you are,
That you are at least happy.

The fact that you are no longer here
Is causing grief so hard to bear,
And it is with a heavy heart, Inay,
That I bid you farewell.
Julian Valdez
01.05.2020
In loving memory of Restituta Caballa y Quiloña — a grandmother whose love was a quiet blessing and whose memory will forever be treasured. May you finally find the peace your beautiful soul deserves in the arms of our Creator, Inay.
