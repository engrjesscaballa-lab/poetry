Today

Today,
The stars have ceased to twinkle
And the moon has lost its glow.

Today,
The usual warmth of the night
Turned into a bitter chill.

Today,
I longed for rain to come and fall,
Endless and unbroken.

Today,
Familiar faces felt far away,
And words were hollow echoes.

Today,
Regret clung to me like a shadow,
While hope slipped out of reach.

All I could wish
Is for today to end
And take with it this ache.
Julian Valdez
