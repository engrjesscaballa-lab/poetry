Yours

Just why is it that you,
Out of all the guys out there,
Have managed to steal my heart,
Never to return it home?
Very earnestly I long for you
And in patience awaits that
Love of yours to call my own.
Accept my affection,
Deny me yours,
Elude me for all I care,
Never, my love, will I
Adore someone else.
Julian Valdez
06.26.2019
Written in youth, before I knew better.
