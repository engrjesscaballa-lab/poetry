The Birth of Spring

Oh, the dreaded day has finally come!
There he is—round glasses, unruly hair.
I never hoped to see that face again
Which but reminds me of torment, of pain.
Of pain! Of friendship lost, of love taken.
But alas, whenever was Fate my friend?
I was sure no sight was more unpleasing
Than seeing that arrogant grin again,
But oh, how my heart was broken
When behind those round glasses of him—
On that hated face
Lied the birth of spring.
Julian Valdez
01.09.2016
A tribute to Professor Severus Snape on his birthday.
